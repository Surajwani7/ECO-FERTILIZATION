FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is Eco-Fertilization?

Eco-Fertilization is an AI-powered platform that provides personalized fertilizer recommendations based on your crop type, location, and weather conditions to optimize crop yields while promoting sustainable farming.

❓ How do I get started?

Simply visit our website, select your crop type, state, and city, and click submit. Our system will analyze the data and provide you with detailed NPK recommendations within seconds.

❓ What is NPK?

NPK stands for Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), and Potassium (K) - the three primary nutrients essential for plant growth. Our recommendations specify the optimal amounts of each for your specific crop.

❓ How accurate are the recommendations?

Our recommendations are based on machine learning models trained on extensive agricultural data, current weather conditions, and scientific research. They are highly accurate and continuously improved.

❓ Can I export my results?

Yes! After getting your recommendations, you can export the detailed report as a PDF file for your records or to share with agricultural consultants.

❓ Is the service free?

Yes, our basic recommendations are completely free. We also offer premium features with enhanced analytics and historical tracking.

❓ Which crops are supported?

We support a wide range of crops including wheat, rice, corn, sugarcane, cotton, soybeans, and many more. New crops are added regularly.

❓ How often should I check for updates?

Weather conditions change frequently. We recommend checking for updated recommendations weekly or whenever significant weather changes occur.

❓ What if I need help?

Visit our Contact page or email us at info@ecofertilization.com. Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM.

❓ Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your information is never shared with third parties without your consent.
